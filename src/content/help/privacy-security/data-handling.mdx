---
title: "How we handle your data"
category: "privacy-security"
order: 1
---

Uply collects only the data necessary to deliver daily questions and track scores. This includes your Slack user ID, display name, workspace ID, question responses, and aggregated scores. We do not access your Slack messages, files, or private conversations.

Your data is hosted on Replit (US-based) and processed via the Slack API. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Appropriate safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses are in place for international data transfers in compliance with GDPR. We follow industry-standard security practices including regular security audits and access controls.

You can request a full export of your personal data at any time by contacting privacy@uply.work. We are committed to transparency about what we store and how we use it.
