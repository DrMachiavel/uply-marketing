Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of, and port your personal data. You also have the right to object to processing and to withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, send an email to privacy@uply.work with your request. We will respond within 30 days as required by law. For data access or portability requests, we will provide your data in a machine-readable format.

If you believe your data rights have not been adequately addressed, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. We take every request seriously and aim to resolve concerns as quickly as possible.
