---
title: "Understanding the weekly leaderboard"
category: "scores"
order: 2
---

Every Monday, Uply resets the weekly leaderboard and posts a summary of the previous week's rankings in the channel you designate. This gives everyone a fresh start and keeps competition friendly and motivating.

The leaderboard ranks all active participants in your workspace by their weekly score. It shows each person's rank, total points earned that week, and their current streak length.

At the end of each week, the top performers are highlighted in the summary message. This recognition is a powerful motivator and helps build a culture of continuous learning within your team.
