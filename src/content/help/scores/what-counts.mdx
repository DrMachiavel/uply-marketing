---
title: "What counts toward your score"
category: "scores"
order: 3
---

Only correct answers to daily questions count toward your score. Each correct answer is worth a base of 10 points, plus any streak bonus you have accumulated from consecutive correct answers.

Unanswered questions and incorrect answers earn zero points. However, simply participating every day — even when you get answers wrong — helps maintain engagement and keeps you in the learning habit.

Scores are calculated the same way across all topics. Whether you are studying Leadership or Communication, the point values and streak mechanics remain consistent, ensuring a fair leaderboard for everyone.
