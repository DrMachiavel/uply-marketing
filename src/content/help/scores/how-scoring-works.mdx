---
title: "How scoring works"
category: "scores"
order: 1
---

Every correct answer earns you points. The base score for a correct answer is 10 points. If you answer correctly on consecutive days, you build a streak that adds bonus points -  the longer your streak, the higher the bonus.

Incorrect answers do not deduct points, but they do reset your streak. The goal is to encourage consistent daily participation rather than punishing mistakes.

Your total score is visible on the Uply app home screen in Slack. It updates in real time after each answer, so you always know where you stand.
