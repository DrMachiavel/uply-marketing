---
title: "Answering questions"
category: "daily-lessons"
order: 2
---

When you receive a daily question, simply tap one of the answer options directly in the Slack message. There is no need to open an external website or app -  everything happens inside Slack.

You have until the end of the day (midnight in your workspace timezone) to answer. If you miss a day, the question expires and you will not earn points for it, but you can still view the correct answer.

After answering, you will see your result immediately along with a short explanation. This instant feedback loop is designed to reinforce learning and help you remember key concepts.
