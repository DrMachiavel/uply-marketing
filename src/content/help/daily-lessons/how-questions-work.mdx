title: "How daily questions work"
category: "daily-lessons"

---

Every weekday, Uply sends you a single question in Slack related to your active topic. Questions are designed to take about two minutes to read and answer, making them easy to fit into a busy schedule.

Each question includes a short scenario or concept followed by multiple-choice options. After you submit your answer, Uply immediately shows whether you were correct along with a brief explanation of the reasoning behind the right answer.

Questions are drawn from a curated library built by soft skills experts. The content covers real workplace situations, research-backed frameworks, and practical techniques you can apply the same day.
