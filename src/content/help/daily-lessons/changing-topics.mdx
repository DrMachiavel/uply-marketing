---
title: "Changing your active topic"
category: "daily-lessons"
order: 3
---

You can change your active topic at any time by opening the Uply app home screen in Slack and selecting a new topic from the list. Your new topic takes effect starting with the next daily question.

Switching topics is useful when you have completed a full cycle on one skill area and want to explore another, or when your role or priorities change. There is no penalty for switching.

Your scores and streaks are tracked per topic, so you can always come back to a previous topic and pick up where you left off. The weekly leaderboard reflects your total score across all topics.
