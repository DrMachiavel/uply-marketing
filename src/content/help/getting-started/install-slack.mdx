---
title: "How to install Uply in Slack"
category: "getting-started"
order: 1
---

Installing Uply in your Slack workspace takes less than a minute. Head to [uply.work](https://uply.work) and click **Get started free**. You will be redirected to Slack's authorization page where you can choose which workspace to install Uply in.

Once you approve the permissions, Uply will appear in your Slack sidebar under **Apps**. You will receive a welcome message with a quick overview of how things work and a prompt to choose your first topic.

If you are not the workspace admin, you may need to request approval from your Slack administrator before the installation can complete. Uply only requests the minimum permissions needed to deliver daily questions and track responses.
