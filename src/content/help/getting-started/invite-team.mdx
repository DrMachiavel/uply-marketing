---
title: "Inviting your team members"
category: "getting-started"
order: 2
---

After installing Uply, you can invite team members by sharing the Uply app link in any Slack channel or by mentioning @Uply in a channel where your team is present. Each person who interacts with Uply for the first time is automatically set up with their own account.

You can also invite people from the Uply dashboard by entering their Slack usernames or email addresses. Invited members will receive a direct message from Uply with instructions to get started.

There is no limit to the number of team members you can invite on any plan. The more people who participate, the more engaging the weekly leaderboard becomes.
