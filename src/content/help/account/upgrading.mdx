---
title: "Upgrading to Pro"
category: "account"
order: 3
---

Upgrading to the Pro plan unlocks unlimited team members, custom topic creation, detailed analytics dashboards, and priority support. To upgrade, open the Uply app home screen in Slack and click **Manage Plan**, then select the Pro tier.

The upgrade takes effect immediately. You will be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your current billing cycle, and then the full Pro rate starting from your next billing date.

If you are not sure whether Pro is right for your team, you can start a 14-day free trial of Pro features. No credit card is required for the trial -  simply select **Try Pro Free** from the plan management page.
