## 1. G2

**Product Name:** Uply

**Category:** Corporate Learning Management Systems (also consider: Employee Training Software, Microlearning Platforms)

**Short Description (150 chars):**
Uply delivers daily 2-minute soft skills training directly in Slack. Scenario-based micro-lessons, leaderboards, and streaks — no new app to adopt.

**Long Description (1000 chars):**
Uply is a Slack-native soft skills training platform that makes leadership development, communication, and team collaboration part of your team's daily routine — in just 2 minutes a day.

Instead of pulling people out of work for workshops or hoping they'll log into yet another LMS, Uply meets teams where they already are: Slack. Every day, each team member receives a scenario-based micro-lesson — a quick question on topics like giving feedback, resolving conflict, managing time, or leading through change. Answers are scored, and weekly leaderboards and streaks keep engagement high through friendly competition.

Uply is built for startups and growing companies (10-100 people) who know soft skills matter but don't have time or budget for traditional training programs. HR leaders, founders, and engineering managers use Uply to build a coaching culture without disrupting flow.

Free for up to 5 users and 1 topic. Pro plans start at $1/user/month — currently free during early access.

**Key Features (5 bullet points):**
- Daily 2-minute micro-lessons delivered directly in Slack — zero context-switching
- Scenario-based questions covering leadership, communication, feedback, collaboration, conflict resolution, and time management
- Weekly leaderboards and streaks that drive consistent engagement through gamification
- No new software to deploy — installs in minutes as a Slack app
- Free tier for small teams; Pro at $1/user/month (free during early access)

**What Makes It Unique:**
Most employee training tools require people to leave their workflow, log into a separate platform, and carve out 30-60 minutes. Uply flips that model entirely. By delivering bite-sized, scenario-based lessons directly inside Slack, Uply removes every adoption barrier. There is no new app to learn, no calendar invite to accept, no LMS to navigate. Teams build real soft skills through daily practice — the same way athletes train — in just 2 minutes a day. The result is higher completion rates, stronger habits, and measurable skill development without disrupting anyone's workday.

**Target Audience:**
Startup founders, HR leaders, engineering managers, and people ops teams at companies with 10-100 employees who want to develop soft skills across their team without the overhead of traditional L&D programs.
