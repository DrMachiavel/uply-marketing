## Implementation Notes

- **Unsubscribe:** Every email must include an unsubscribe link (legal requirement + respect).
- **From address:** Use a personal name (e.g., "Elliot from Uply" or "Elliot at Uply") — not "Uply Team" for emails 2-4. Email 1 can come from "Uply" since it's transactional.
- **Reply-to:** Route to a monitored inbox. Replies from this sequence are high-intent.
- **Segmentation:** If a lead installs Uply at any point, remove them from this sequence and move to an onboarding sequence.
- **A/B testing:** Test subject lines in batches of 100+ before committing. Prioritize open rate for Email 1 (it sets the tone), click rate for Emails 2-4.
- **Timing:** Send at 9-10 AM in the recipient's local timezone (Tuesday-Thursday preferred for B2B).
